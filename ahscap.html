Communication Design for Possible Futures

This arts & humanities capstone project is an exercise in "reality creation". The project consists of two phases: 1) creating graphic artifacts based on the speculative or imagined works by other designers, and 2) creating a pitch deck and realistic "looks like" prototype of an original product concept. Please see process book and deliverables for full details.
